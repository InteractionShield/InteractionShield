Bandera is a open-source tool kit for model-checking concurrent Java software.  Bandera is developed and maintained primarily by the researchers at the SAnToS Laboratory at Kansas State University and supported by signficant collaboration with researchers from the Automated Software Engineering Group at NASA Ames and the LASER Group at the University of Massachussetts at Amherst.

Bandera is distributed under a General Public License (GPL).

For current information about the Bandera project, please consult the project URL (http://www.cis.ksu.edu/bandera).
